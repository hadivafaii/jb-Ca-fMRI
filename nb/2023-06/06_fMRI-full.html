Note on parcel.df#

We have both id and region columns. id means region id, something to extract the ROI masks from parcellation results. These are unique. In other words, each ROI has a unique id. On the other hand, region has some sort of degenracy. For instance, there are only 128 regions, but with different layer and hemisphere ids for cortex. As another example, there are only 72 unique regions for HIP, which means 144 unique ids in total. See below.
